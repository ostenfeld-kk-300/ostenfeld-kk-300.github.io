Year of Founding

Bongostan was founded in 1991.

About the Founding

Bongostan was established with a vision of unity and prosperity. It has grown into a unique micronation with a rich history and vibrant culture.

The secession from Denmark was remarkably smooth, primarily because we did not inform them of our departure.

  • East Timor (2002)
  • Palau (1994)
  • Eritrea (1993)
  • Czech Republic (1993)
